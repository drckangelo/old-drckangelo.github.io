Glances, Augmented-Reality based Face-recognition app

Description

  • Derick David

  • Glances Official

  • June, 2017

Naples-based start-up that develops an augmented-reality based app that uses face-recognition to recognise and discover people. The app will simply give you the power to connect and discover people around you by just pointing it. It will then show the demographics and the social networks he/she possesses. Our backbone is the Facial-Recognition algorithm we developed entirely from scratch without any use of libraries. From this we will build Augmented-Reality technology on top of it showing the user the coolest and smooth experience ever. My role for our start-up is Chief Marketing Officer and Front-end Web Developer thus I was the one who created our landing page.

Technology

  • Swift for iOS Development
  • ARKIT
  • Objective-C
  • Cocoa Touch Frameworks
